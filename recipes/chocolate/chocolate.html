Peppermint Hot Chocolate

Description :

A warm drink that is sure to warm you on a cold morning!

Ingredients :

  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 1 peppermint candy cane

Steps :

  1. Stir 2 tablespoons milk, cocoa powder, sugar, cinnamon, vanilla extract together in a mug until well blended.

  2. Microwave 3/4 cup milk on high for 2 1/2 minutes in a microwave-safe mug. Pour the hot milk over the cocoa mixture; stir with candy cane.
